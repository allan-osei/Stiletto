How to Add Our Bot to a Telegram Channel or Group

To add a bot to your Telegram channel or group and give it permissions to post messages, follow these steps:

  1. Start the bot: Tap on this link and press start. @ToTchatbot

  2. Add Bot to Channel/Group: Add the bot to your channel or group as you would add any member. In the case of a channel, make sure to add it as an administrator.

  3. Set Permissions: Go to the channel or group settings, find 'Administrators', select your bot, and set its permissions. Enable 'Post Messages' for channels or 'Send Messages' for groups.

  4. Start Using Your Bot: Now your bot is ready to send messages to your channel or group. You can customize its messages using your webhookk's parse option.

Ensure that your bot has the necessary permissions to perform its tasks, and you're all set!
