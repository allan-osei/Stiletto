MetaTrader5


MetaTrader 5 is a free application for traders allowing to perform technical analysis and trading operations in the Forex and exchange markets.




How It Works


Step 1

Create a webhook in your dashboard.

Step 2

Activate your hook on our payment processor.

Step 3

Attach the Webhook to your TradingView alerts along with the desired message.

Step 4

Download our expert advisor and attach it to your terminal
Step 5

Send alerts to the mt5 terminal and execute trades.

Step 6

Switch between different channels and groups anytime.

Trading Parameters


Mandatory

  • Action: Instructions to be carried out when an alert is triggered. Example: buy, sell, close, or modify a trade.
  • Symbol: The name of the symbol to apply the action to. For example; EURUSD.

Optional

  • Quantity (Q): The size of the order in lot size or percentage. Example: Q=0.1 or Q=10%.
  • Price (P): The entry price of your order, converting your trade to a stop or limit order. Example; P=2%, P=1.1234
  • TakeProfit (TP): Add the take profit of a trade, specified in points, price, or percentage from the entry price.
  • StopLoss (SL): Add the stop loss of a trade, specified in points, price, or percentage from the entry price.
  • Trailing Trigger (TT): The point at which your trailing stop loss for open trades will trigger, specified as points or as a percentage.
  • Trailing Distance (TD): The distance between your stop loss and the current market price, specified in points or as a percentage.
  • Trailing Step (TS): The number of points/percentage movement in the market after which your SL should be adjusted.
  • Magic (M): The trade magic number to use, to identify different strategies. Example M=1234.
